This page allows you to request notices to appear on the system

Display Date (Required)

Enter the date on which your notice should appear - format "yyyy/mm/dd"

Group/Section (Required)

Checkbox

Specify whether the notice belongs to a specific group (preferably, to utilise all features of this app) or to a generic section

Select

Click to choose from a list of groups or sections

Title (Optional if group chosen above, Required if section)

Enter the title of your notice to a limit of 100 characters

Description (Required)

Enter the description of your notice to a limit of 1000 characters

Tag Students (Optional)

Begin typing the name of a student to mention A dropdown will appear allowing you to select this student You may mention as many students as you need to

Submission button

Click to request a notice with the above details. If successful, the form will reset, otherwise you will be informed of an error
