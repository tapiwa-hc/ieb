This page displays all the notice groups on the system and allows you to edit, add, and delete these groups. In all cases, the list will update if successful, otherwise you will be informed of an error.

Each list-item

  • Click "Edit" to change the name
    • Click "Save" to save changes
    • Click "X" to discard changes
  • Click "Delete" to attempt to remove group from system
    • If there are still notices under this group, you will be warned and prompted to continue - doing so will remove these notices as well

New Group Form

Click "+" floating button in bottom right corner of list to open the form

  • Click "X" to close form OR
  • Fill both the fields
  • Click "Submit" to push the new group onto the database

Search

This page supports Chrome's 'find' feature. Simply press CTRL+F to begin searching
